**Problem Statement**: Determine the number of 4-letter words that can be formed using the letters A, B, C, D, and E, where each word must contain at least one vowel (A or E), letters can be repeated, and any combination is considered a valid word (not limited to English words).

---

**Step 1**

[PLANNING]  
Action: Forward Subgoaling (Planning)  
Break down the problem into intermediate subgoals to compute the number of valid 4-letter words. Subgoals:  
1. Calculate the total number of possible 4-letter words using the given letters.  
2. Calculate the number of 4-letter words with no vowels.  
3. Subtract the number of words with no vowels from the total to find words with at least one vowel.  
[/PLANNING]

---

**Step 2**

[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)  
Current Goal: Calculate the total number of possible 4-letter words using letters A, B, C, D, and E with repetition allowed.  
How to Achieve: Since each position in the 4-letter word can be any of the 5 letters (A, B, C, D, E), compute the number of choices per position and multiply across all positions.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
Each of the 4 positions in the word can be filled with any of the 5 letters (A, B, C, D, or E). Thus, the total number of 4-letter words is:  
\[ 5 \times 5 \times 5 \times 5 = 5^4 = 625 \]  
So, there are 625 possible 4-letter words.  
[/IMP]

---

**Step 3**

[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)  
Current Goal: Calculate the number of 4-letter words with no vowels.  
How to Achieve: Identify the consonants (B, C, D) and compute the number of 4-letter words using only these letters, with repetition allowed.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
The consonants are B, C, and D (3 choices). Each of the 4 positions must be filled with one of these consonants. Thus, the number of 4-letter words with no vowels is:  
\[ 3 \times 3 \times 3 \times 3 = 3^4 = 81 \]  
So, there are 81 words with no vowels.  
[/IMP]

---

**Step 4**

[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)  
Current Goal: Calculate the number of 4-letter words with at least one vowel.  
How to Achieve: Subtract the number of words with no vowels from the total number of words to find those with at least one vowel.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
Total 4-letter words: 625 (from Step 2).  
Words with no vowels: 81 (from Step 3).  
Words with at least one vowel:  
\[ 625 - 81 = 544 \]  
So, there are 544 words with at least one vowel.  
[/IMP]

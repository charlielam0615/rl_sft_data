### Step 3

Description: Substitute \( g(x) = x - 1 \) into \( f(x) = \frac{3x^2 + 5x + 8}{x^2 - x + 4} \).
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
Substitute \( x \) in \( f(x) \) with \( g(x) = x - 1 \):
\[ f(g(x)) = f(x - 1) = \frac{3(x - 1)^2 + 5(x - 1) + 8}{(x - 1)^2 - (x - 1) + 4}. \]

Numerator:
\[ (x - 1)^2 = x^2 - 2x + 1, \]
\[ 3(x - 1)^2 = 3(x^2 - 2x + 1) = 3x^2 - 6x + 3, \]
\[ 5(x - 1) = 5x - 5, \]
\[ 3(x - 1)^2 + 5(x - 1) + 8 = (3x^2 - 6x + 3) + (5x - 5) + 8 = 3x^2 - 6x + 3 + 5x - 5 + 8 = 3x^2 - x + 6. \]

Denominator:
\[ (x - 1)^2 = x^2 - 2x + 1, \]
\[ -(x - 1) = -x + 1, \]
\[ (x - 1)^2 - (x - 1) + 4 = (x^2 - 2x + 1) + (-x + 1) + 4 = x^2 - 2x + 1 - x + 1 + 4 = x^2 - 3x + 6. \]

Thus:
\[ f(g(x)) = \frac{3x^2 - x + 6}{x^2 - 3x + 6}. \]

Evaluate at \( x = 1 \):
Numerator: \( 3(1)^2 - 1 + 6 = 3 - 1 + 6 = 8 \),
Denominator: \( (1)^2 - 3(1) + 6 = 1 - 3 + 6 = 4 \),
\[ f(g(1)) = \frac{8}{4} = 2. \]
[/IMP]

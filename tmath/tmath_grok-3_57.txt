[IMP]  
The Asymptote code provides the path: \((-4, -5) \to (-2, -1) \to (-1, -2) \to (1, 2) \to (2, 1) \to (4, 5)\). Since the function is continuous, we assume \( f(x) \) is defined over the intervals determined by the x-coordinates of these points. We calculate the equation of each line segment using the slope formula \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \) and the point-slope form \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \).

1. **Segment 1: \((-4, -5)\) to \((-2, -1)\), \( x \in [-4, -2] \)**  
   Slope: \( m = \frac{-1 - (-5)}{-2 - (-4)} = \frac{4}{2} = 2 \)  
   Equation using \((-4, -5)\):  
   \( y - (-5) = 2(x - (-4)) \)  
   \( y + 5 = 2(x + 4) \)  
   \( y = 2x + 8 - 5 = 2x + 3 \)  
   So, \( f(x) = 2x + 3 \) for \( x \in [-4, -2] \).

2. **Segment 2: \((-2, -1)\) to \((-1, -2)\), \( x \in [-2, -1] \)**  
   Slope: \( m = \frac{-2 - (-1)}{-1 - (-2)} = \frac{-1}{1} = -1 \)  
   Equation using \((-2, -1)\):  
   \( y - (-1) = -1(x - (-2)) \)  
   \( y + 1 = -(x + 2) \)  
   \( y = -x - 2 - 1 = -x - 3 \)  
   So, \( f(x) = -x - 3 \) for \( x \in [-2, -1] \).

3. **Segment 3: \((-1, -2)\) to \((1, 2)\), \( x \in [-1, 1] \)**  
   Slope: \( m = \frac{2 - (-2)}{1 - (-1)} = \frac{4}{2} = 2 \)  
   Equation using \((-1, -2)\):  
   \( y - (-2) = 2(x - (-1)) \)  
   \( y + 2 = 2(x + 1) \)  
   \( y = 2x + 2 - 2 = 2x \)  
   So, \( f(x) = 2x \) for \( x \in [-1, 1] \).

4. **Segment 4: \((1, 2)\) to \((2, 1)\), \( x \in [1, 2] \)**  
   Slope: \( m = \frac{1 - 2}{2 - 1} = \frac{-1}{1} = -1 \)  
   Equation using \((1, 2)\):  
   \( y - 2 = -1(x - 1) \)  
   \( y = -x + 1 + 2 = -x + 3 \)  
   So, \( f(x) = -x + 3 \) for \( x \in [1, 2] \).

5. **Segment 5: \((2, 1)\) to \((4, 5)\), \( x \in [2, 4] \)**  
   Slope: \( m = \frac{5 - 1}{4 - 2} = \frac{4}{2} = 2 \)  
   Equation using \((2, 1)\):  
   \( y - 1 = 2(x - 2) \)  
   \( y = 2x - 4 + 1 = 2x - 3 \)  
   So, \( f(x) = 2x - 3 \) for \( x \in [2, 4] \).

Thus, the piecewise function is:
\[
f(x) =
\begin{cases} 
2x + 3 & \text{if } -4 \leq x \leq -2 \\
-x - 3 & \text{if } -2 < x \leq -1 \\
2x & \text{if } -1 < x \leq 1 \\
-x + 3 & \text{if } 1 < x \leq 2 \\
2x - 3 & \text{if } 2 < x \leq 4 
\end{cases}
\]

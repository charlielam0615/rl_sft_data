- Total students: 32
- Students with a dog: \( \frac{1}{2} \cdot 32 = 16 \)
- Students with a cat: \( \frac{3}{8} \cdot 32 = 12 \)
- Students with other pets: 6
- Students with no pets: 5
- A Venn diagram with:
  - Cats only: 10
  - Dogs only: 2
  - Other pets only: 9
  - Cats and dogs, not other: \( y \)
  - Cats and other, not dogs: \( x \)
  - Dogs and other, not cats: \( w \)
  - All three pets: \( z \)

The goal is to find \( z \), the number of students with all three pets.

### Problem Restatement
We need to find the positive integer \( k \) such that the value of the expression \((3^{1001} + 4^{1002})^2 - (3^{1001} - 4^{1002})^2\) equals \( k \cdot 12^{1001} \).

### Step 2: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)

Current Goal: Simplify the expression \((3^{1001} + 4^{1002})^2 - (3^{1001} - 4^{1002})^2\).

How to Achieve: Recognize that the expression is a difference of squares, \((a + b)^2 - (a - b)^2\), where \( a = 3^{1001} \) and \( b = 4^{1002} \). Apply the identity \((a + b)^2 - (a - b)^2 = 4ab\) to simplify it.
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
Let \( a = 3^{1001} \) and \( b = 4^{1002} \). The expression is:

\[
(a + b)^2 - (a - b)^2
\]

Using the identity \((a + b)^2 - (a - b)^2 = 4ab\):

\[
(a + b)^2 - (a - b)^2 = 4 \cdot a \cdot b = 4 \cdot 3^{1001} \cdot 4^{1002}
\]

Now compute \( 4 \cdot 3^{1001} \cdot 4^{1002} \):

- Note that \( 4 = 2^2 \), so \( 4^{1002} = (2^2)^{1002} = 2^{2 \cdot 1002} = 2^{2004} \).
- Thus:

\[
4 \cdot 3^{1001} \cdot 4^{1002} = 4 \cdot 3^{1001} \cdot 2^{2004}
\]

- Since \( 4 = 2^2 \):

\[
4 \cdot 2^{2004} = 2^2 \cdot 2^{2004} = 2^{2006}
\]

- So the expression becomes:

\[
2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001}
\]

[/IMP]

### Step 3: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)

Current Goal: Express \( 2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001} \) in the form \( k \cdot 12^{1001} \).

How to Achieve: Rewrite the expression by factoring it to include \( 12^{1001} \), where \( 12 = 2^2 \cdot 3 \). Adjust the exponents and identify the remaining factor as \( k \).
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
We need:

\[
2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001} = k \cdot 12^{1001}
\]

Since \( 12 = 2^2 \cdot 3 \), we have:

\[
12^{1001} = (2^2 \cdot 3)^{1001} = (2^2)^{1001} \cdot 3^{1001} = 2^{2 \cdot 1001} \cdot 3^{1001} = 2^{2002} \cdot 3^{1001}
\]

Now express \( 2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001} \):

\[
2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001} = 2^{2002 + 4} \cdot 3^{1001} = (2^{2002} \cdot 3^{1001}) \cdot 2^4 = 12^{1001} \cdot 2^4
\]

Since \( 2^4 = 16 \):

\[
2^{2006} \cdot 3^{1001} = 12^{1001} \cdot 16
\]

Comparing with \( k \cdot 12^{1001} \):

\[
k = 16
\]

[/IMP]

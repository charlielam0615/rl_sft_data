### Step 12: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Current Goal**: Implement a technology break schedule, including phone-free hours each day (Subgoal 5).  
**How to Achieve**: Develop a schedule with designated phone-free hours, using tools like app timers or physical phone storage to enforce breaks, complementing outdoor activities.
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
Create a technology break schedule with 2–3 hours of phone-free time daily, such as 5:00–7:00 PM, which can overlap with outdoor activities like the 30-minute routine or soccer practice. Use a phone timer app (e.g., Forest or Digital Detox) to lock the device during these hours. Alternatively, place the phone in a designated “off-limits” area at home during breaks. Encourage activities like journaling or family time to fill these hours, reducing texting dependency.
[/IMP]

### Step 8: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Current Goal**: Provide emotional support by listening attentively.  
**How to Achieve**: Be present during conversations, listening without judgment and acknowledging their feelings.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
When the friend wants to talk, listen actively without interrupting. Show empathy by nodding, maintaining eye contact, and responding with phrases like, “That sounds really tough—I’m here for you.” Avoid offering unsolicited advice; instead, ask open-ended questions like, “How are you feeling about everything?” to encourage them to share. Schedule regular check-ins, such as a weekly call, to maintain consistent support.  
[/IMP]

**How to Achieve**: Research reputable divorce lawyers and provide the friend with recommendations or assist in contacting them.

[IMP]

### Step 9: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data**:  
- **Current Goal**: Allow the glaze to dry.  
- **How to Achieve**: Place the tile in a suitable environment to dry the matte glaze completely, following manufacturer guidelines.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
Place the glazed tile in a well-ventilated, dust-free area at room temperature (approximately 20–25°C). Refer to the matte glaze manufacturer’s instructions, which typically recommend 24–48 hours for air drying. Avoid touching the tile or exposing it to moisture during this period. If firing is required (common for ceramic glazes), place the tile in a kiln and fire at the recommended temperature (e.g., cone 06, around 1000°C) for the matte glaze to set properly. Ensure the kiln is set for a slow cooling cycle to prevent cracking. The tile is complete once the glaze is fully dry or fired.  
[/IMP]

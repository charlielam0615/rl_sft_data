### Step 9

Lay the T-shirt flat on a cutting surface, smoothing out any wrinkles. ### Step 10
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:** The current goal is to cut the T-shirt fabric according to the pattern. This will be achieved by cutting around the pinned pattern pieces to create the fabric pieces for the dress.

[IMP]  
Using fabric scissors, carefully cut the T-shirt fabric along the edges of the pinned pattern pieces. Follow the pattern precisely, including any notches or alignment marks. ### Step 11
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:** The current goal is to sew the side seams of the fabric. This will be achieved by using a sewing machine to join the fabric pieces along the side seams.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
Align the front and back fabric pieces with right sides together, matching the side seams. Pin the side seams to hold them in place. Using a sewing machine with a stretch or zigzag stitch (suitable for T-shirt fabric), sew along the side seams with the designated seam allowance (e.g., 1/2 inch). Remove the pins and check the seams for strength and evenness. Trim any excess threads.  
[/IMP]

### Step 12
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:** The current goal is to hem the dress if desired. This will be achieved by folding and sewing the bottom edge of the dress to create a clean hem.

[IMP]  
Decide if a hem is desired based on the sketch and desired length. If hemming, fold the bottom edge of the dress inward by about 1/2 inch, press it flat, and pin it in place. Using the sewing machine with a stretch or zigzag stitch, sew the hem close to the folded edge. Remove the pins and check that the hem is even and secure. If no hem is desired, skip this step and proceed.  
[/IMP]

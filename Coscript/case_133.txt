[IMP]  
To adjust DPI settings:

- On macOS: Go to System Settings > Displays, adjust the "Resolution" to a scaled option that effectively lowers DPI.  
- Confirm the new settings by clicking "Apply" or "OK."  
This step reduces the DPI, leveraging the improved posture for better focus during the process.

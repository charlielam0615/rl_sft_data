### Step 11: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]  
**Action**: Reach Goal (Implementation)

[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
Establish a markdown policy: items unsold after 30 days are discounted by 10%, after 60 days by 20%, and after 90 days by 30%, ensuring prices stay within the competitive 5-10% market range. For example, a $50 dress (market average $40-$60) would be marked down to $45 after 30 days, $40 after 60 days, and $35 after 90 days. Update the inventory system to track item age and automate markdowns. Inform consignors of the policy to maintain transparency. Monitor sales to assess markdown effectiveness.  
[/IMP]

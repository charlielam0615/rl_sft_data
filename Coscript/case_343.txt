### Step 9: Reach Goal (Implementation)

[PLANNING]
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Current Goal**: Secure the streamers tightly to the ceiling.  
**How to Achieve**: Use strong adhesive hooks, tape, or ceiling clips to fasten streamers at the marked points, ensuring they remain taut and secure throughout the event.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
To secure the streamers, attach them to the marked points using removable adhesive hooks or heavy-duty double-sided tape suitable for the ceiling material (e.g., drywall or wood). For each streamer, fasten one end to a marked point, pull it taut across the ceiling to the opposite point, and secure the other end. Test the tension by gently tugging each streamer to ensure it doesn’t sag or detach. For a 20x30 ft ceiling with 10-12 streamers, use at least 20-24 hooks (one per end). If the venue has a drop ceiling, consider using ceiling clips for easier installation. Double-check that all attachments are firm to withstand the duration of the wedding event.  
[/IMP]

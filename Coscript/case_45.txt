### Step 7: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**

[/PLANNING]  

[IMP]  
To prepare the chocolate layer:  
- In a small saucepan, melt 4 ounces (115 grams) of semisweet chocolate over low heat, stirring constantly to prevent burning.  
- Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in ¼ cup (60 milliliters) of condensed milk until smooth.  
- Allow the chocolate mixture to cool for 2-3 minutes to avoid melting the pudding layer during assembly.  
[/IMP]

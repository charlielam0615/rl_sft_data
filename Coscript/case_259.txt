**Action**: Reach Goal (Implementation)

[IMP]  
Schedule an OGTT with a healthcare provider. Fast for 8-12 hours before the test. During the test, a baseline blood glucose level is measured, followed by consumption of a 75-gram glucose drink. Blood glucose is then measured at intervals (typically 1 and 2 hours) to assess how effectively the body processes glucose. Normal 2-hour glucose levels are below 140 mg/dL; levels of 140-199 mg/dL suggest impaired glucose tolerance, and 200 mg/dL or higher may indicate diabetes.  
[/IMP]

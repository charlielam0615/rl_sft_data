### Step 6: Reach Goal (Implementation)

[PLANNING]  
**Action**: Reach Goal (Implementation)  
**Meta-data**:  
- **Current Goal**: Remove the cake from the refrigerator and allow it to reach room temperature.  
- **How to Achieve**: Take the cake out of the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for an appropriate amount of time to ensure optimal texture and flavor before serving.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
To achieve the goal of preparing the cake for serving:  
1. Remove the cake from the refrigerator, keeping it in its container or wrapping to avoid contamination.  
2. Place the cake on a counter in a clean, dry area at room temperature (approximately 68°F to 72°F or 20°C to 22°C).  
3. Allow the cake to sit for 1-2 hours, depending on its size, to reach room temperature. This prevents condensation and ensures the fondant and cake texture are optimal for serving.  
4. Unwrap the cake carefully after it has reached room temperature to avoid damaging the fondant.  
[/IMP]

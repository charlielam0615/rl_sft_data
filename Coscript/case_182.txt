[/PLANNING]

[IMP]  
To ensure the video is high quality:  
- **Video Resolution**: Verify the video is at least 1080p for clear visuals, as higher resolution is more appealing to viewers.  
- **Audio Clarity**: Ensure audio is clear, with no background noise, using a good microphone or post-production editing tools like Audacity.  
- **Content Engagement**: Confirm the video has a compelling introduction, clear narrative, and professional editing (e.g., using software like Adobe Premiere or Final Cut Pro) to retain viewer attention.  
- **Length**: Keep the video concise (ideally 3-5 minutes) unless the content justifies a longer duration, as shorter videos tend to retain viewers.  
These actions align with the goal of maximizing views by enhancing viewer experience.

[IMP]

Alternatively, research common Bluetooth kits (e.g., FM transmitter-based or AUX-based kits) to determine if they are universally compatible with the car’s audio setup. ### Step 8: Reach Goal
[PLANNING]
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:**  
- **Current Goal:** Install the Bluetooth kit in the car.  
- **How to Achieve:** Follow the kit’s installation instructions, connecting it to the car’s audio system and ensuring proper setup.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]
Unbox the Bluetooth kit and review its manual for specific installation steps. For an AUX-based kit, plug the receiver into the car’s AUX port, connect it to a power source (e.g., USB port or cigarette lighter adapter), and secure it in a convenient location (e.g., dashboard or center console). For an FM transmitter kit, plug it into the cigarette lighter, select an unused FM frequency on both the kit and the car’s radio, and ensure a clear signal. If the kit requires professional installation (e.g., wired integration with the car’s stereo), schedule an appointment with a car audio technician. Test the kit by powering it on and ensuring it is operational before proceeding to pairing.  
[/IMP]

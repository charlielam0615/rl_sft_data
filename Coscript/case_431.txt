### Step 6
[PLANNING]
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:** The current goal is to practice active and patient listening. This will be achieved by giving full attention during conversations, using verbal and non-verbal cues to show engagement, and allowing pauses for the loved one to process their thoughts.  
[/PLANNING]

[IMP]

Summarize their points occasionally, e.g., “It sounds like you’re feeling frustrated about your progress,” to show understanding. This fosters a supportive environment and strengthens emotional connection.

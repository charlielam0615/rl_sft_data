**Current Goal**: Avoid using harsh chemicals for bathroom cleaning.  
**How to Achieve**: Identify and eliminate the use of cleaning products containing harsh chemicals like bleach, ammonia, or chlorine by reviewing product labels and replacing them with safer alternatives.

[IMP]  
To achieve the goal of avoiding harsh chemicals in bathroom cleaning, start by auditing current cleaning products.

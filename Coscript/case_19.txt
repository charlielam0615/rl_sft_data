### Step 3
[PLANNING]  
**Action: Reach Goal (Implementation)**  
**Meta-data:**  
- **Current Goal:** Ensure sufficient sleep to support physical and mental health.  
- **How to Achieve:** Develop a sleep schedule targeting 7-8 hours per night, create a bedtime routine to promote relaxation, and optimize the sleep environment (e.g., dark, quiet room).  
[/PLANNING]  

[IMP]  
To achieve sufficient sleep:  
- Set a consistent sleep schedule, aiming to sleep from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM daily, providing 8 hours of rest.  
- Create a bedtime routine: 30 minutes before bed, avoid screens, read a book, or practice light stretching to signal the body to wind down.  
- Optimize the sleep environment: Use blackout curtains, keep the room at a cool temperature (around 65°F), and minimize noise with earplugs or a white noise machine if needed.  
- Avoid caffeine or heavy meals within 4 hours of bedtime to prevent sleep disruption.  
This plan aligns with the constraint of staying healthy by promoting restorative sleep, which supports physical recovery and mental clarity in the Work World context.

[IMP]  
To apply a coat of latex primer, first gather the necessary materials: a paint sprayer, latex primer suitable for brick surfaces, protective gear (gloves, goggles, mask), and drop cloths or plastic sheeting to protect surrounding areas. Test the sprayer on a small, inconspicuous area to ensure the spray pattern and pressure are correct. Begin applying the primer in a systematic pattern, working from top to bottom in small sections to avoid drips. Maintain a consistent distance (about 8-12 inches) from the surface for even coverage. Overlap each pass slightly to prevent missed spots. Ensure the entire brick surface is covered with a thin, even coat. Address any uneven areas immediately by re-spraying as needed.

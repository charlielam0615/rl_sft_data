**Step 15:**  
[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation Category)  
Current Goal: Turn on two-factor authentication (Subgoal 7)  
How to Achieve: Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in the account security settings to add an extra layer of protection for the Etsy account.  
[/PLANNING]  
[IMP]  
Log in to your Etsy account and navigate to “Account Settings,” usually accessible via your profile icon or menu. Look for the “Security” or “Privacy & Security” section. Find the option for two-factor authentication (2FA) and click to enable it. Etsy typically offers 2FA via text message or an authenticator app. Choose your preferred method—if selecting text, enter your mobile number and verify it with a code sent to your phone; if using an app, download an authenticator like Google Authenticator, scan the QR code provided by Etsy, and save the setup key. Once activated, 2FA will require a second form of verification during login, securing your shop from unauthorized access.  
[/IMP]

**Step 16:**  
[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation Category)  
Current Goal: Set up your storefront (Subgoal 8)  
How to Achieve: Customize the Etsy shop’s appearance and settings by accessing the shop manager to define the shop name, banner, policies, and other branding elements.  
[/PLANNING]  
[IMP]  
In your Etsy account, go to “Shop Manager” from the left-hand menu or dashboard. Click on “Settings” or “Shop Settings” to begin customizing your storefront. Choose a unique and memorable shop name that reflects your brand (ensure it’s available by checking Etsy’s suggestions). Upload a shop banner (recommended size often 3360x840 pixels) and logo or icon (400x400 pixels) that visually represent your business. Write a compelling shop announcement and define shop policies, including shipping, returns, and payment terms, tailored to Indian regulations and customer expectations. Save all changes to finalize your storefront’s look and feel, making it ready to attract buyers with a professional appearance.  
[/IMP]

**Step 17:**  
[PLANNING]  
Action: Reach Goal (Implementation Category)  
Current Goal: Add more listings to your shop (Subgoal 9)  
How to Achieve: Create and upload product listings to the Etsy shop by providing detailed descriptions, photos, pricing, and shipping information for each item.  
[/PLANNING]  
[IMP]  
From the “Shop Manager” dashboard, select “Listings” and click “Add a Listing” or “Create a Listing.” For each product, upload high-quality photos (up to 10 images per listing, with the first as the thumbnail; recommended size 2000x2000 pixels for clarity). Write a detailed title (up to 140 characters) using relevant keywords for searchability, and provide a comprehensive description covering materials, dimensions, and usage. Set the price, considering Etsy fees (listing fee of $0.20 per item and transaction fee of 6.5% as of current standards) and your profit margin. Specify shipping options, including costs and estimated delivery times for India and international buyers. Tag the listing with relevant keywords (up to 13 tags) to improve visibility. Save and publish each listing, repeating the process to add multiple products to diversify your shop’s offerings.  
[/IMP]

Engage with online communities or forums related to your niche (e.g., handmade crafts or Indian artisans) to build a customer base. Offer introductory discounts or free shipping for Indian buyers to incentivize first purchases. Consistent promotion increases shop traffic and potential sales.

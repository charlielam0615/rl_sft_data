### Step 8
[PLANNING]  
**Action**: Reach Goal (Implementation)  
**Meta-data**:  
- **Current Goal**: Check the float valve on the sump pump.  
- **How to Achieve**: Test the float valve to ensure it moves freely and activates the pump correctly.  
[/PLANNING]  
[IMP]  
Access the float valve on the sump pump. Manually lift and lower the float to confirm it moves without resistance. Adjust or replace the float valve if it does not function properly.

**Action**: Reach Goal (Implementation)

[/PLANNING]  
[IMP]

Route the pipe along the pre-planned path to the exterior drainage point, ensuring a slight upward slope to facilitate water flow. Secure the pipe to the wall or floor to prevent movement.  
[/IMP]

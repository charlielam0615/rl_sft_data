[IMP]  
Start with the given equation:  
\[ x^2 + y^2 + 3 = -4y + 6x - 1 \]  

Move all terms to one side:  
\[ x^2 + y^2 + 3 + 4y - 6x + 1 = 0 \]  
\[ x^2 - 6x + y^2 + 4y + 4 = 0 \]  

Complete the square for \( x \):  
\[ x^2 - 6x = (x - 3)^2 - 9 \]  

Complete the square for \( y \):  
\[ y^2 + 4y = (y + 2)^2 - 4 \]  

Substitute back:  
\[ (x - 3)^2 - 9 + (y + 2)^2 - 4 + 4 = 0 \]  
\[ (x - 3)^2 + (y + 2)^2 - 9 = 0 \]  
\[ (x - 3)^2 + (y + 2)^2 = 9 \]  

This is the equation of a circle with center \( (3, -2) \) and radius \( r = \sqrt{9} = 3 \).  
[/IMP]
